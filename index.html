About

Kevin DSouza is a Creative UX Graphic Designer, Illustrator and Front-End Developer, A Bachelor of Arts & Graphic Design from Becker College, USA.

He has over 10 Years of Experience as a Creative Graphic Designer and an Exciting Portfolio in Digital Marketing and Advertising. He provides User Experience Design for Web-Sites, Mobile Devices, I-pads, and In-Store Touch Panels.
